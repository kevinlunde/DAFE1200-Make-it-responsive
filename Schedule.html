What's an Innovation Camp?

Here at Inova we love to bring new and promising ideas to life. In our quest to do so, we invite people from all around the world to participate in our innovation camps. The camps are usually based around frugal innovation, as it is what we specialize in and can provide help and support for our participants. If you choose to take part in the camps, you will both be at the right place and meet the right people.

When all places for a camp has been filled, we sort it out and set up teams that we think will do well and get along with eachother. And these teams will be revealed at the opening day for the innovation camp. When all teams have been put together and have met up we will explain the issue in question and let the groups work by themselves. We have of course rented rooms for each individual group, which will be given at the start of the day. Throughout the camp we will be holding seminars about the problem and do Q&A's for those interested, other than that you get to dispose the time however the group see fit.

Usually at the last day of the camp each group will present their idea or solution, and a jury who is well educated within the subject will judge and pronounce the three best solutions. The winners will receive a price which will vary from camp to camp. The main focus is to use your ability to the fullest and be innovative!
