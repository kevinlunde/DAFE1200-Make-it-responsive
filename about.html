About Inova

Inova AS works with arranging and organizing innovation camps and seminars for young entreupeneurs and innovative minds. We are currently based in Oslo, Norway, but do have plans to have our seminars and innovation camps arranged in more cities in the future. Our goal is to provide students and young innovators with a valuable experience to kickstart their studies and career.
